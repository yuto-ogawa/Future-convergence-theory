## Future Authenticity Art: A Protocol for Unique Certification by Integrated Hash in Line Drawings

### Abstract



This paper introduces the concept of Future Authenticity Art, an innovative art form that embeds hash values, pen pressure, stroke patterns, and semantic density into physical line drawings. The protocol enables the artwork itself to function as a self-verifying entity beyond traditional NFT-based ownership models. We present the systematic production methods, self-verification mechanisms, and future recovery procedures, establishing a foundational framework for authenticating art across time. This work marks the first proposal of a truly future-convergent authenticity art protocol
