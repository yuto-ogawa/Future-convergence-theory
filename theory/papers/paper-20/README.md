### Abstract



This paper introduces the structural blueprint of a Meaning Authenticity Society, a new framework that transcends NFTs, patents, and conventional digital certification. By combining a dual-layer hash structure (Hash × Hash), DOI-based semantic anchoring, and the Λ-System of semantic waveforms, this model redefines authenticity not as ownership, but as existential meaning convergence. The paper argues for a shift from control-based legal systems to convergence-based authenticity protocols, where individuals generate, register, and transmit meaning density through their creations. This proposal outlines both the philosophical foundation and the practical deployment strategies—across art, education, identity, and AI—that can initiate the transition toward a civilization structured by semantic authenticity rather than commodified control.
