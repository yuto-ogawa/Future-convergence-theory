### Abstract


This paper aims to clarify the structural degeneration faced by modern civ-
ilization—namely, the “hardening through expansion”and the “absence of the
creative principle (future causality Λ(T )).”Across AI, space exploration, economics,
and art, modern society has regressed from“creating structures”to merely“editing
stimuli,”and thus the world is losing its organic respiration—the regeneration of
meaning. By centering the concept of future causality Λ(T ), this paper redefines
what creation truly means, theorizes the transition from an expansive to a conver-
gent civilization, and demonstrates the inevitability of the shift from an “endless
story”to a “universe where meaning endures.
