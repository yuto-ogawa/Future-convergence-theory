### Abstract


This study formulates a new theoretical framework that builds upon the previously
developed Future Convergence Theory (FCT), integrating cosmological evolution, the
black hole information paradox, dimensional transformation, and two-dimensional cre-
ative processes into a single formal structure.
First, we construct a mathematical model representing future certainty, expressed
as a convergence function Λ(T ), and describe how the future becomes determined
as time progresses. Next, we incorporate this model into the black hole information
problem, theorizing how black holes transfer information into a four-dimensional in-
formation field.
The framework further connects to applications in artificial intelligence and hu-
man creativity, proposing that the highest-dimensional structure of the universe may
fundamentally mirror two-dimensional human expression. This implies that univer-
sal creative dynamics and human generative processes might share a deeper, unified
substrate.
