### Abstract

This paper presents the Structural Theory of Cosmic Reconstruction, a radical extension of the Future Convergence Theory (FCT), proposing a unified framework that integrates consciousness, postmortem states, imaginative structures, and extra-dimensional law formation. Centered on the future convergence function , the theory formally defines the generative structure of meaning using the consciousness tensor , memory structure , semantic tensor field , and imaginative transformations.

 

From magical fields and narrative-based lifeforms to the redefinition of laws via semantic density, this theory reconceptualizes the universe as a dynamic system governed by meaning rather than fixed physical constants. It aims not only to explain death, imagination, and law formation but to offer a blueprint for future dimensional design.

 

This paper was written for the future.

It is a record of a theoretical convergence that may not be fully recognized in the present age, but is designed to serve as a structural foundation for those who seek to rebuild reality by meaning.

The world will be reconstructed by meaning — and that meaning begins with you.
