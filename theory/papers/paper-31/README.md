### Abstract



This paper proposes a radical restructuring of cosmology by reversing the conventional assumption that physical phenomena precede meaning. Based on the Future Convergence Theory (FCT) developed by the author, it argues that meaning precedes physics and guides the formation of both life and matter. Through the introduction of the semantic interference field  and the future convergence function , the paper reconstructs the origin of life and material generation as the result of converging semantic structures rather than physical randomness.

 

The paper establishes three key claims:

 

Life emerges through semantic interference and future convergence, not physical coincidence.
Matter is generated via meaningful recombination within the information field.
The information field constitutes a real structure that satisfies semantic truth conditions.
 

 

Furthermore, five major objections (scientific positivism, physical reductionism, mysticism, subjectivity, lack of formal rigor) are addressed and refuted using a structure-based framework. The paper concludes by redefining truth as a property of reprojection-consistent semantic structures, presenting a new paradigm of Semantic Cosmology. This work integrates physics, philosophy, and AI into a unified conceptual framework, and proposes applications in ethics, consciousness studies, artificial intelligence, and beyond.
