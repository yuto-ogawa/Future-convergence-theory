## Future Convergence Theory: A New Framework for Time and Future Certainty

### Abstract

In this study, we propose a new theoretical framework called “Future Convergence Theory,” which redefines the relationship between causality and the flow of time. This theory aims to integrate traditional determinism and probabilistic approaches by considering the possibility that future states influence present decisions. As a challenge to conventional causality, we analyze how uncertainties in the future can affect present choices using a mathematical model. This paper formalizes the concept of future convergence and discusses its potential applications. Furthermore, we explore its implications in various fields, including physics, economics, and artificial intelligence.
