### Abstract


This paper presents a paradigm-shifting framework that redefines existence not through material or observational criteria, but through semantic convergence from the future. Positioned beyond Galileo, Newton, and Einstein, the work formalizes the Λ(T) function, introduces the concept of semantic density, and implements the Λ-System and Meaning Authenticity Society. Through these structures, the author establishes a new ontological and epistemological foundation where irreproducibility becomes evidence of authenticity and the future actively selects the present through meaning. This is not a theoretical proposition alone, but a structural design of civilization itself—marking the first human attempt to architect the semantic basis of reality.
