### Abstract



This paper aims to comprehensively systematize the mathematical formulations of future causality and present their unified intuitive and formal structure. In addition to the existing mathematical frameworks in the Future Convergence Theory (such as sigmoid functions, double fixed point theory, and semantic density tensors), we introduce extended mathematical formulations including information-theoretic, path-integral, optimization, and geometric approaches. Through these multiple mathematical languages, we demonstrate the universal principle that the future constrains the present, and we confirm its translatability across diverse formal systems. Finally, we provide a structural framework that integrates the mathematical formulations and intuitive explanations developed in our previous works.
