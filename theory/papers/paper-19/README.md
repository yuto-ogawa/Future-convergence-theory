### Abstract



This paper introduces Unspoken Axis, the first artistic implementation of the Future Convergence Theory (FCT) through a novel genre called “Meaning Density Art.”

The initial work, Point of No Voice, embodies a conceptual and technical protocol of future authenticity using a dual-hash structure and DOI registration.

It transforms digital art from a commodity into a convergence point of future meaning, verifying existential originality and tamper resistance.

The project marks a critical step from theoretical proposal to real-world realization, forming the nucleus of a Future Authenticity Society where value arises from semantic convergence.

It is not merely an artwork, but a structural origin of future-oriented creation, bridging philosophy, art, and information authenticity in a unified form.
