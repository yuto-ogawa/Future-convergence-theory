### Abstract


This paper presents a new theoretical framework that redefines the essence of evolution by introducing the concepts of “semantic density” and the future convergence function , departing from Darwinian evolution based on natural selection. While traditional evolution theory is grounded in past adaptation, this theory proposes that the gradient of semantic density and future-directed causal structure drive transformations across all hierarchical levels: organisms, species, civilizations, and even the universe. Using the semantic density tensor  and the future information function , the theory unifies descriptions of individual free will, species-level selection, institutional evolution, and cosmic structural generation. This work integrates biology, physics, information theory, and ethics into a forward-looking, meaning-centered paradigm of evolution.
