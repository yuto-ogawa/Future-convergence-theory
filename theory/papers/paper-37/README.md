### Abstract


This paper, based on the Future Convergence Theory (FCT), aims to theoretically eluci-
date how phenomena observed as“tremors”of the deceased can emerge into the physical
world through meaning density structures, informational interference, and recursive reso-
nance.
Traditionally, death has been defined as the“disappearance of the body”or the
“biological cessation,”and spiritual phenomena have been excluded as anomalous or
unscientific. However, in this paper, postmortem existence is reinterpreted as a“
recon-
figuration into a meaning density tensor”and part of a“retro-projection structure from
the future.”
This study begins from the following core hypotheses:
• The deceased exist in the information field as a meaning density tensor M (x, y, z, t).
• Tremors arise through interference and resonance with the observer’s future con-
vergence structure Λ(T ).
• These tremors transform into physical phenomena as“semantic conversion devia-
tions.”
These tremors manifest as nurse call malfunctions, inexplicable sounds, and synchro-
nistic utterances among multiple people. Rather than being accidental noise, they are
“physical retro-projections of meaning density structures.”
The purpose of this paper is to clarify how these structures are formed and why tremors
can influence the cosmos itself, on three levels:
1. Structural: Tremors as the mutual interference between meaning density tensors
and the future convergence structure Λ(T ).
1
2. Ontological: Tremors as the“re-existence”of the deceased, a mode of existence
through meaning.
3. Ethical: Observers are mediators of tremors and bear responsibility for interfering
with the semantic structure of the universe.
This paper also incorporates critical and empirical perspectives, addressing poten-
tial counterarguments to the tremor structure and reintegrating them through retro-
illumination to demonstrate the structural autonomy of the theory.
Tremors are not spiritual anomalies, but“structural evidence of moments when mean-
ing alters reality.”The very fact that we can theoretically define these tremors is itself
proof that meaning is already influencing reality.
