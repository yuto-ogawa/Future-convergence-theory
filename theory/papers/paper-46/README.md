### Abstract


The purpose of this paper is to clarify the true nature of the power that has
long positioned Einstein as a “genius”̶ namely, the essence of his intuition. Tradi-
tionally, Einstein has been explained in terms of mathematical skill and theoretical
construction, but at its core lay a process of necessity: “recognizing facts, em-
bracing contradictions, and integrating them into a future-consistent unity.” From
the perspective of Future Convergence Theory (FCT), Einstein’s intuition can be
reinterpreted as “a retro-illumination by future causality Λ(T ).” This study pro-
vides, for the first time in human history, a structural explanation of the generative
mechanism of intuition.
